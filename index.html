Our Services

At Khayrmawaaye Petroleum Company, we provide reliable and efficient energy solutions for individuals, businesses, and industries. From quality fuel supply and bulk delivery to modern retail stations and sustainable energy initiatives, we are your trusted partner for dependable service and lasting value.

Fuel Supply & Distribution

We ensure a steady and reliable supply of high-quality petrol, diesel, and kerosene to businesses, industries, and retail partners. Our strong logistics network guarantees timely delivery wherever it’s needed.

Retail Service Stations

We operate modern, well-managed fuel stations designed for safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. Each station upholds our commitment to quality service and product integrity.

Bulk Fuel Delivery

For corporate and industrial clients, we provide customized bulk fuel delivery services that meet specific operational needs — from transport fleets to construction sites.

Lubricants & Oils

We supply premium automotive and industrial lubricants that enhance engine performance and equipment durability. Our range is suitable for vehicles, machinery, and heavy-duty applications.

LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas)

We offer safe, efficient LPG solutions for households, businesses, and industries — ensuring clean and reliable energy for everyday use.

Dealer & Partnership Programs

Entrepreneurs and investors can partner with us through our dealership and franchise programs, benefiting from our brand reputation and operational expertise.

About Us

Khayrmawaaye Petroleum Company is a trusted and growing energy provider committed to delivering reliable, high-quality petroleum products that keep businesses and communities moving. Guided by integrity and innovation, we ensure consistent product quality, efficient logistics, and dependable service while investing in cleaner technologies and strong partnerships that drive a sustainable energy future. Our work is grounded in three core values ;

  • Excellence.
  • Safety.
  • Sustainability.
